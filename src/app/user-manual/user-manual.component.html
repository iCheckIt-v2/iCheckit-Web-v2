User Manual

Task Management

In adding a task:

  • Navigate to the main dashboard.
  • Click on the add icon located at the bottom-right of the page.
  • Once a modal or a pop-up has appeared, enter the necessary credentials for the details of the task that will be added.
  • If the entered task scope is wrong or needs to be edited, click on the Clear Scope button and enter the new task scope.
  • To sucessfully add the task, click on the Add Task button.

In deleting a task:

  • Navigate to the main dashboard.
  • Click on the task that needs to be deleted.
  • Navigate to the task settings ()
  • Once navigated, click on the trash bin located under the logout button.
  • A pop-up will appear, stating a warning that the task will be deleted. To fully delete the chosen task, click on Delete Task.

In updating the details of a task:

  • Navigate to the main dashboard.
  • Click on the task that needs to be updated.
  • Navigate to the task settings ()
  • Once navigated, the information about the task will be displayed.
  • The fields that are editable about the details of the task are as follows: Task Title, Task Description, and the Task Status.
  • To apply certain changes on these fields, click on the Update Task button.

Regarding the submission of the students:

  • Navigate to the main dashboard.
  • Click on a specific task.
  • Under the tasks section, all of the students included in the task scope will be displayed, as well as their status for submission.
    • If the students did not submit their proof of completion yet, their status will remain as Pending.
    • If the students did not submit their proof of completion yet and the deadline is long overdue, their status will change to Late.
    • If the students did submit their proof of completion, their status will be For Approval since it is up for the admins to approve/reject their submission, according to their standards.
    • If the students did submit their proof of completion and it was accepted by the admins, their status will be Accomplished.
    • If the students did submit their proof of completion but it was rejected by the admins, their status will go back as Pending.

Regarding the submission of the students:

  • Navigate to the main dashboard.
  • Click on a specific task.
  • Click on the pie chart icon () to view the data on how many have submitted their proof of completion for that specific task.
